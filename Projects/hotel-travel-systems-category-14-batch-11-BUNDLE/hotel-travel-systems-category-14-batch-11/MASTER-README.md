# Hotel & Travel Systems — Category 14 / Batch 11

This batch contains 20 independent Next.js + TypeScript + Tailwind CSS frontend templates for hotel and travel systems.

## Templates

1. **LuxeStay Resort Booking** — `luxestay-resort-booking` — Five-star resorts and luxury hotel chains — Resort rooms, spa packages, VIP guest portal, revenue dashboard
2. **TripCraft Travel Agency** — `tripcraft-travel-agency` — Travel agencies and tour operators — Tour packages, itinerary builder, inquiry booking, consultant dashboard
3. **SuiteDesk Hotel PMS Front** — `suitedesk-hotel-pms-front` — Boutique hotels and serviced apartments — Availability grid, booking flow, guest portal, room operations dashboard
4. **NomadPass Adventure Tours** — `nomadpass-adventure-tours` — Adventure tour companies — Expedition packages, risk notes, booking slots, itinerary tracker
5. **SkyBridge Flight & Hotel** — `skybridge-flight-hotel` — Online travel agencies and package sellers — Bundles, flight/hotel cards, booking checkout mock, trip dashboard
6. **MedinaHajj Pilgrimage Planner** — `medinahajj-pilgrimage-planner` — Hajj/Umrah agencies and religious travel operators — Group packages, itinerary, rooming list mock, operations dashboard
7. **CoastalVilla Rental Portal** — `coastalvilla-rental-portal` — Villa rental companies and property managers — Villa listings, availability, booking, guest account, owner dashboard
8. **CityBreak Hotel Deals** — `citybreak-hotel-deals` — City hotels and deal aggregators — Deal cards, filters, last-minute offers, checkout mock, account page
9. **CruiseHarbor Booking** — `cruiseharbor-booking` — Cruise companies and marine travel agencies — Cabin packages, route itinerary, passenger booking, cruise dashboard
10. **SafariBase Expedition Travel** — `safaribase-expedition-travel` — Safari operators and destination management companies — Camp packages, guide profiles, availability, itinerary, booking request
11. **BusinessStay Corporate Travel** — `businessstay-corporate-travel` — Corporate travel departments and B2B agencies — Policy-aware booking, traveler profiles, approval dashboard, reports
12. **HostelHive Budget Stays** — `hostelhive-budget-stays` — Hostel chains and budget travel platforms — Dorm listings, beds, traveler reviews, booking, guest hub
13. **WellnessRetreat Planner** — `wellnessretreat-planner` — Wellness resorts and retreat organizers — Retreat programs, schedule, practitioner cards, guest portal
14. **EventStay Group Blocks** — `eventstay-group-blocks` — Conference hotels, event planners, and venues — Group room blocks, attendee booking, allocation dashboard, deadlines
15. **DestinationPro DMC Portal** — `destinationpro-dmc-portal` — DMCs and inbound travel operators — Vendor cards, itinerary management, bookings, client portal, ops dashboard
16. **Aparthotel FlexStay** — `aparthotel-flexstay` — Aparthotels and serviced residence operators — Long-stay plans, room types, rates, booking, resident portal
17. **EcoTrail Sustainable Travel** — `ecotrail-sustainable-travel` — Eco lodges and sustainable travel companies — Eco packages, impact metrics, booking, guide notes, traveler portal
18. **RailVoyage Packages** — `railvoyage-packages` — Rail tourism companies and travel agencies — Routes, cabins, schedule, itinerary, booking flow, traveler dashboard
19. **IslandHop MultiDestination** — `islandhop-multidestination` — Island resorts and multi-city tour companies — Multi-stop itinerary, transport legs, hotel nights, booking summary
20. **TravelOps Control Tower** — `travelops-control-tower` — Large travel agencies and operations teams — Ops dashboard, bookings, vendors, issues, traveler notifications

## Batch Scope
Each template includes marketing pages plus system-style screens: packages, availability, booking flow, guest portal, and operations dashboard.

## Run Any Template
```powershell
cd "template-folder"
.\run.ps1
```

## Build Any Template
```powershell
.\build.ps1
```

## Zip Any Template
```powershell
.\zip.ps1
```
