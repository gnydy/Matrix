# Portfolio Index — Category 15 Logistics & Delivery Systems

Use these cards in the main MatrixAll portfolio marketplace.

- **RoutePulse Delivery OS** — A command center for daily delivery operations, failed attempts, driver allocation, and route health. Target: Last-mile delivery startup.
- **FleetNova Logistics Control** — A fleet performance interface focused on vehicle utilization, driver shifts, maintenance, and dispatch efficiency. Target: Fleet-based logistics company.
- **CargoGate Freight Portal** — A B2B freight portal for shipment requests, customs milestones, carrier options, and document readiness. Target: Freight forwarding company.
- **CourierDesk Express** — A simple but premium courier dashboard for pickup requests, live tracking, SLA alerts, and customer communication. Target: Local courier service.
- **ColdChain Monitor** — A specialized logistics template for temperature-sensitive shipments, sensor alerts, route deviation, and compliance reports. Target: Cold-chain delivery provider.
- **WarehouseDispatch Hub** — A warehouse dispatch front-end for outbound waves, pick-pack status, loading bays, and courier handoff. Target: Warehouse and dispatch operation.
- **ShipMatrix 3PL Portal** — A client-facing 3PL portal for orders, inventory movement, billing snapshots, tickets, and SLA performance. Target: Third-party logistics provider.
- **RiderFlow Food Delivery** — A rider operations template for restaurant pickup, dispatch zones, rider availability, and delivery exceptions. Target: Food delivery platform.
- **ParcelTrack Customer Portal** — A customer tracking portal with tracking number lookup, shipment milestones, support requests, and delivery preferences. Target: Parcel delivery company.
- **DriverOps Mobile Control** — A driver management dashboard for shifts, onboarding, compliance, documents, and performance coaching. Target: Driver-heavy transport operation.
- **PortLink Container Tracking** — A container tracking interface for port milestones, vessel ETA, demurrage risk, documents, and consignee updates. Target: Shipping and container logistics company.
- **MetroRoute Public Delivery** — A dense city logistics interface for delivery zones, courier density, traffic risk, and live dispatch balancing. Target: Urban delivery operator.
- **ReturnLoop Reverse Logistics** — A reverse logistics template for return requests, inspection steps, pickup scheduling, refund stages, and warehouse disposition. Target: E-commerce returns operation.
- **QuickShip Marketplace Logistics** — A marketplace logistics backoffice for seller shipments, courier allocation, failed orders, and customer claims. Target: Marketplace shipping team.
- **HeavyHaul Project Logistics** — A project logistics interface for heavy loads, permits, escort vehicles, route constraints, and staged delivery milestones. Target: Heavy equipment transport company.
- **PharmaRoute Medical Logistics** — A pharma logistics UI for batch tracking, delivery proof, route compliance, cold chain alerts, and recipient verification. Target: Medical distribution company.
- **AirCargo Ops Board** — A cargo airline or airport cargo interface for airway bills, flight capacity, loading status, and customs release. Target: Air cargo operation.
- **GreenFleet Carbon Logistics** — A green logistics dashboard for route efficiency, carbon estimates, EV fleet status, and client sustainability reports. Target: Sustainable logistics provider.
- **MultiBranch Dispatch HQ** — A headquarters-level dashboard for branches, dispatch capacity, local SLAs, revenue by region, and operational exceptions. Target: Multi-branch delivery company.
- **DeliveryPay COD Control** — A COD-focused logistics template for collected cash, unsettled orders, courier balances, reconciliations, and risk alerts. Target: Cash-on-delivery logistics company.
