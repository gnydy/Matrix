# Portfolio Index — Real Estate Platforms

- **EstateVista Luxury Brokerage** — Luxury brokerage platform for premium homes, villas, and penthouses. Target: Luxury real estate agencies and high-ticket brokers. Use case: Showcase premium property inventory with white-glove viewing requests and elite agent profiles.
- **UrbanNest Rentals Platform** — Fast rental search platform for apartments, studios, and managed rentals. Target: Rental marketplaces and property management startups. Use case: Help tenants filter rental units and request viewings quickly.
- **CompoundHub Egyptian Developments** — New development sales platform for compounds and gated communities. Target: Developers and real estate sales teams. Use case: Present compounds, phases, amenities, payment plans, and lead capture.
- **PropInvest ROI Marketplace** — Investment-focused property marketplace with yield and ROI indicators. Target: Property investors, brokers, and advisory firms. Use case: Compare investment opportunities using rent yield, appreciation, and risk scores.
- **VillaSphere Coastal Homes** — Coastal villas and vacation homes platform with lifestyle-led browsing. Target: Vacation home brokers and coastal developers. Use case: Sell coastal villas through galleries, lifestyle sections, and booking previews.
- **PrimeOffice Commercial Real Estate** — Commercial real estate platform for offices, coworking, and business spaces. Target: Commercial brokers and corporate leasing teams. Use case: List offices with capacity, lease terms, amenities, and corporate viewing requests.
- **LandMark Plots & Land Portal** — Land and plots portal for developers, farmers, and investors. Target: Land brokers, developers, and investment offices. Use case: Display land parcels with zoning, infrastructure, frontage, and acquisition workflow.
- **RentEase Tenant Portal** — Tenant-facing rental portal with maintenance and lease information. Target: Property managers and rental operators. Use case: Let tenants view leases, pay mock invoices, submit maintenance, and book visits.
- **NewCapital Projects Gallery** — High-conversion gallery for New Capital projects and units. Target: Egyptian real estate marketers and project sellers. Use case: Present project inventory, towers, delivery dates, and viewing requests.
- **AgentFlow Brokerage CRM** — Brokerage CRM interface for agents, leads, listings, and follow-ups. Target: Brokerage teams needing operational UI templates. Use case: Show agent pipelines, client requests, listings, tasks, and lead status.
- **HomeAuction Bids Portal** — Auction-style property portal with bids, timers, and offer tracking. Target: Auction houses and distressed asset brokers. Use case: Show bid-ready properties, reserve prices, viewing slots, and bid activity mock.
- **SmartEstate AI Matchmaker** — AI-assisted real estate discovery interface with recommendation cards. Target: Modern agencies and AI-enabled property startups. Use case: Match buyers with units using lifestyle, budget, location, and priority filters.
- **ServicedStay Aparthotel Listings** — Serviced apartment and aparthotel listing platform. Target: Hospitality operators and serviced residence brands. Use case: List short-stay apartments with amenities, availability, and guest services.
- **WarehouseGate Industrial Properties** — Industrial real estate portal for warehouses, factories, and logistics yards. Target: Industrial brokers and logistics companies. Use case: Compare warehouse capacity, loading docks, truck access, and lease terms.
- **GreenHabitat Eco Homes** — Eco-friendly homes platform with energy and sustainability indicators. Target: Green developers and sustainability-focused brokers. Use case: Promote eco homes using energy ratings, solar readiness, and lifestyle benefits.
- **MortgageBridge Property Finance** — Mortgage-first property discovery and finance calculator platform. Target: Mortgage advisors, banks, and broker partnerships. Use case: Let buyers calculate payments, compare plans, and request finance-ready units.
- **PropManage Owner Portal** — Owner portal for property portfolios, revenue, maintenance, and tenants. Target: Property managers and landlord service companies. Use case: Show owner dashboards, unit performance, maintenance requests, and occupancy.
- **StudentNest Housing** — Student housing platform for shared apartments and campus-friendly rentals. Target: Student housing operators and rental agencies. Use case: Help students search by campus distance, roommate rules, budget, and availability.
- **MallSpace Retail Leasing** — Retail leasing platform for malls, plazas, and storefronts. Target: Mall operators and commercial leasing teams. Use case: Show shop spaces, footfall, unit sizes, categories, and leasing inquiries.
- **GlobalRelocate Expat Homes** — Relocation property platform for expats, executives, and international families. Target: Relocation firms and premium rental brokers. Use case: Offer relocation-ready homes with school proximity, services, and concierge viewings.
